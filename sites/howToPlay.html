HOW TO PLAY

DECK

DECK

YOUR DECK HOLDS ALL THE CARDS YOU HAVE UNLOCKED AND DISPENCES A RANDOM ONE ON YOUR HAND IF THERE ARE EMPTY SLOTS

HAND

YOUR HAND CAN HOLD UP TO 5 CARDS AT A TIME, WHICH YOU CAN PLACE IN AN ACTIVE SLOT IF YOU HAVE ENOUGH ORBS

HAND

10

HEALTH

IF YOU DO NOT HAVE ANY ACTIVE CARDS YOUR OPPONENT CAN ATTACK YOUR HEALTH. IF IT REACHES 0 YOU LOSE

ACTIVES

YOUR 4 ACTIVE SLOTS ARE THE SLOTS WHERE YOU PLACE YOUR CARDS TO ATTACK OR DEFEND AGAINST YOUR OPPONENT

ACTIVE

10

ORBS

ORBS ARE GENERATED AT THE BEGINGING OF EACH ROUND AND ARE USED TO PLACE CARDS IN YOUR ACTIVE SLOTS

ABILITIES

SOME CARDS CAN HAVE ABILITIES THAT ARE EITHER USED WHEN PLACED OR CAN BE MANUALLY ACTIVATED DURING YOUR TURN
